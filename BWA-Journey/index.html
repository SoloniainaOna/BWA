Step 01 — Explore
Explore
the space
Before anything else, take time to simply be in the room. Move freely, observe at your own pace — there is no right order.
  • 1
    Walk through the entire room without rushing. Let your eye follow what draws you.
  • 2
    Observe each work from multiple distances — up close, then from across the room.
  • 3
    Notice how light, scale, and placement shape your perception.
  • 4
    Try every interactive element you can find in the space.
  • 5
    When you feel ready, tap Finished to move on.

Step 03 — VR
Virtual
reality
When you're ready, pick up one of the VR headsets available in the room. A member of staff is here to help you if needed.
  • 1
    Pick up a headset from the designated area and put it on.
  • 2
    Confirm the user selection by aiming at it, and press the Volume + button.
  • 3
    You can look around freely. Take your time inside the experience.
  • 4
    When finished, remove the headset and place it back carefully.
  • 5
    Return to this tablet and tap Finished to continue.

Step 04 — App
Open
the app
Head to the top floor to discover the paintings. You can open the Artivive app and point your phone at the artworks to reveal hidden layers, or simply enjoy them as a classic exhibition — take your time and let each piece speak for itself.
  • 1
    Go up to the top floor where the paintings are displayed.
  • 2
    Open the Artivive app and point your camera at the artworks to see them come alive — or simply admire them as they are.
  • 3
    When you're done, come back here and tap Finished.
